Encapsulation
-----------------------------
- One of the basic pillars of Object Oriented Programming language.
- Encapsulation is nothing but wrapping up of data(variables) and code(methods) together as a single unit. ex: Medicine Capsule 
- Hiding the data or Data Security
- Data refers to (data members of a class) variables of a class.
- We can make data members as Read-Only or Write-Only
